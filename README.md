**CFPMonitor** is a comparison website that tracks Call for Papers (CFP) submission policies and review rules across 300+ computer science conferences.

**[Browse the live website &rarr;](https://ZhongkuiMa.github.io/CFPMonitor/)**

## What You Can Find

- **Submission deadlines** -- abstract, full paper, camera-ready, and notification dates
- **Review policies** -- double-blind review, rebuttal, page limits, formatting requirements
- **Submission rules** -- concurrent submission, dual submission, supplementary materials
- **Emerging policies** -- LLM/AI-assisted writing policies, artifact evaluation, ethics review
- **Conference metadata** -- dates, locations, submission systems (OpenReview, HotCRP, etc.)

All information is extracted from publicly available conference websites, [ccf-deadlines](https://ccfddl.github.io/), and [WikiCFP](http://www.wikicfp.com/), then structured into a filterable, searchable interface.

## Coverage

- **321 conferences** across 10 research areas: AI, Software Engineering, Security, Databases, Networking, Computer Architecture, Graphics, HCI, Theory, and Interdisciplinary
- **3 ranking systems**: CCF, CORE, THCPL
- **Tracked years**: 2024--2026

## Disclaimer

CFPMonitor is an **unofficial, community-maintained** project. All information is derived from publicly available sources. This project does not rank or recommend conferences, does not provide submission advice, and does not guarantee accuracy or completeness. **Always verify with official conference websites.**
